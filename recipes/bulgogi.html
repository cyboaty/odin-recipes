Bulgogi

What is Bulgogi?

Bulgogi is barbecued marinated sliced beef.

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • green onions
  • yellow onion
  • sugar
  • garlic, minced
  • sesame seeds
  • sesame oil
  • gochugaru
  • fresh ginger, minced
  • black pepper
  • sirloin steak, sliced thinly
  • honey

How to Prepare

  1. Whisk soy sauce, green and yellow onions, sugar, garlic, sesame products, gochugaru, ginger, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  2. Add steak and evenly coat in the marinade, let sit for up to a day.
  3. Stir in steak and marinade together in a pre-heated skillet, adding honey to caramelize the steak, working in batches until all steak is fully cooked.
  4. Finish with green onion on top.

Kimchi Fried Rice

What is Kimchi Fried Rice?

It's an easy-on-the-wallet dish utilizing rice and kimchi.

Ingredients

  • oil
  • your choice of meat
  • green onion
  • kimchi (duh)
  • rice (duh)
  • gochujang
  • egg
  • butter

How to Prepare

  1. Cook the meat and whites of the green onion in oil.
  2. Add kimchi and gochujang, then stir with rice.
  3. Cook to heat the rice.
  4. Fry an egg on top, finish with sesame oil and green onion.

Miso Soup

What is Miso Soup?

Miso soup is a Japanese soup made from soybeans.

Ingredients

  • water
  • dashi
  • miso paste
  • tofu, diced
  • green onion

How to Prepare

  1. Combine water and dashi, bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce to medium heat, add miso paste.
  3. Add diced tofu.
  4. Finish with green onion.
